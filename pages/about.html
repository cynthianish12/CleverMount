Our story
Clever Mount School is a nursery school that was found in the year of 2022. The school was started by Tumba Coffee LTD under three main objectives which were:
  • - Proving Tumba Coffe workers with Education services for their kids

  • - Helping poor children in the commiunity to get access to Education

  • - Providing Education services to the community
    • Clever Mount receives children from 3 years to five years where by it has there classes
  • - Baby class (3 years)

  • - Middle class (4 years)

  • - Top class (5 years)
